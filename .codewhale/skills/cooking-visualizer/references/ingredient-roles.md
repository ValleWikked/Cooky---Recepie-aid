# Ingredient Roles Reference

Use this file when reasoning about Panel 1 content: filling the 6-column quantity
matrix AND the "Варианты и замены" section for each ingredient.

The 6 columns are: **0 | ½× | ¾× | 1× | ⁵⁄₄× | 3/2×**

For each ingredient category below, the table gives the verdict and consequence
for each column. The "Zone" field describes where the tolerance zone lies:
WIDE = several adjacent columns give effectively the same result;
NARROW = even one step from 1× is perceptible.

Mark flour and any empirically-determined quantities with `?` at 1×.
Mark estimated air fryer times with `~`.

## Aromatics — Лук (onion)

Role: sweetness, aromatic depth, moisture contribution to raw mixtures.

Zone: WIDE below 1× (¾× often indistinguishable from 1×). MODERATE above
(⁵⁄₄× starts introducing excess moisture in patty/ball mixtures).

| Column | Verdict | Consequence |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 0      | Плоский вкус | No sweet backbone. The dish tastes one-dimensional — the base ingredient dominates without aromatic support. |
| ½×     | Слабый фон | Subtle hint of sweetness. Texture better in raw mixtures (less moisture). Noticeably mild to most palates. |
| ¾×     | Баланс ✓ | Effectively the same result as 1× for most people. Slightly less sweet, but within the tolerance zone. |
| 1×     | Баланс ✓ | Correct sweetness and aromatic depth. Moisture contribution is accounted for in the recipe. |
| ⁵⁄₄×   | Начинает водянить | In raw mixtures (falafel, veggie burgers): onion releases extra liquid → mixture becomes wetter → more binder needed → denser result. In cooked dishes: less critical. |
| 3/2×   | Водянисто | Clear excess moisture in raw mixtures. Significantly more binder required to compensate. Onion flavour starts to dominate. |

**Form variants:**
- Fresh: standard. Releases moisture when cut or processed — factor this into binding.
- Dried/powder (луковый порошок): use ~1 ч.л. instead of 80 г. No moisture contribution — mixture will be drier, may need less or no binder. Flavour more concentrated and uniform.
- Шалот (shallot): more delicate, slightly sweeter, less moisture. Same quantity or 10% more.
- Зелёный лук (scallion): milder, less sweet. Use white and light-green parts only, ~60 г instead of 80 г.
- Красный лук (red onion): sharper raw flavour, more colour. Same quantity.

**Substitutions:**
- Шалот: closest substitute, slightly more refined flavour.
- Луковый порошок: works if moisture reduction is acceptable.
- Омission: viable in falafel — the dish still works, just less sweet depth.

## Aromatics — Чеснок (garlic)

Role: pungency, aromatic depth, amplifies spices.

Zone: WIDE below 1× (¾× to 1× barely distinguishable). MODERATE above (⁵⁄₄× starts to overpower spices).

| Column | Verdict | Consequence |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 0      | Нет остроты | Flavour is softer and more neutral. Spices taste less amplified. |
| ½×     | Едва заметен | Faint background note. Most people will not notice the difference from zero. |
| ¾×     | Лёгкий аромат | Noticeable but subtle. Spices come through cleanly without competition. |
| 1×     | Выражен ✓ | Clear garlic note without dominating. Amplifies cumin and coriander effectively. |
| ⁵⁄₄×   | Усилен | Garlic becomes noticeable as its own flavour, starts to compete with spices. After cooking: less pronounced than raw. |
| 3/2×   | Резкий | Garlic dominates. In raw mixture the sharpness is aggressive; cooking softens it but the imbalance with spices remains. |

**Form variants:**
- Fresh cloves: standard. Sharpest, most complex flavour.
- Чесночный порошок: use ½ ч.л. instead of 3 cloves. No moisture, evenly distributed, milder and more uniform flavour. Lacks fresh garlic's volatile bite.
- Жареный чеснок (roasted): much sweeter and milder. Use 5–6 roasted cloves instead of 3 raw. Changes the character significantly — less пряный, more sweet.
- Чесночное масло (garlic oil): useful for flavour without texture; not suitable for falafel mixture.

**Substitutions:**
- Чесночный порошок at reduced quantity: acceptable workaround.
- Omission: acceptable. Flavour becomes milder.
- No direct flavour substitute exists for fresh garlic's specific volatile compounds.

## Fresh Herbs — Петрушка / Кинза

Role: freshness, bright green colour on cross-section, flavour contrast to earthy base.

Zone: WIDE below 1× for flavour (omitting is acceptable); NARROW above 1× for moisture.

| Column | Verdict | Consequence |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 0      | Нет зелени | Interior is beige-brown. Flavour is earthier and heavier. The dish is still functional — omission is acceptable. |
| ½×     | Бледно-зелёный | Faint herbal note. Colour barely visible on cross-section. |
| ¾×     | Лёгкая свежесть | Visible green flecks. Flavour present but not assertive. Within tolerance zone for most recipes. |
| 1×     | Зелёный срез ✓ | Clear green flecks visible. Fresh contrast to earthy chickpeas. Herb flavour noticeable but balanced. |
| ⁵⁄₄×   | Активная зелень | Herb flavour starts to compete with spices. Mixture slightly wetter. |
| 3/2×   | Зелень доминирует | Herbal taste overpowers spices. Mixture noticeably wetter → may require additional binder. Interior becomes very green. |

**Form variants:**
- Свежая (fresh): standard. Contributes moisture and bright colour. Refrigerate and use within 3 days of purchase for best colour retention.
- Сухая (dried, as a spice): use ~1½ ч.л. instead of 25 г (ratio ~1:4 by volume). No moisture contribution, no green colour on cross-section. Flavour is more concentrated but less fresh. Acceptable in a pinch — noticeably different.
- Замороженная (frozen): behaves like fresh once thawed but releases more water — blot thoroughly dry before use.

**Substitutions for петрушка:**
- Кинза (cilantro): same quantity. Traditional in Levantine-style falafel. More citrusy and assertive — divisive flavour, but authentic.
- Укроп: use ~15 г instead of 25 г. Significantly different flavour profile (more Eastern European character). Not traditional in falafel.
- Basil: NOT recommended for falafel — flavour clash with cumin/coriander.
- Omission: fully acceptable. The dish works without herbs.

**Substitutions for кинза:**
- Петрушка: same quantity. Milder, less citrusy.
- Петрушка + small amount of lime zest (~½ ч.л.) can approximate cilantro's brightness.

## Dry Spices — Кумин (зира / cumin)

Role: defines the Levantine/Middle Eastern character of falafel. The primary identity spice.

Zone: NARROW in both directions. This is a sensitive ingredient.

| Column | Verdict | Consequence |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 0      | Нет характера | Falafel loses its identity. Tastes like seasoned chickpeas, not falafel. The dish is structurally fine but culturally unrecognisable. |
| ½×     | Мягкий вкус | Faint cumin note. Suitable for those who dislike assertive spices. Lacks depth. |
| ¾×     | Умеренный | Recognisable but restrained. Coriander may come forward more. Acceptable for sensitive palates. |
| 1×     | Аутентично ✓ | Correct Levantine profile. Works in balance with coriander — neither dominates. |
| ⁵⁄₄×   | Начинает горчить | Cumin begins to tip toward bitterness, especially at air fry temperatures. The balance with coriander shifts. |
| 3/2×   | Горечь | Clear bitter note from cumin. Overpowers coriander. The dish becomes unpleasantly astringent. |

**Form variants:**
- Pre-ground from packet: standard. Functional but loses potency after ~6 months of opening. Sniff the packet — if the aroma is faint, use more.
- Freshly ground from toasted whole seeds: noticeably more aromatic. Method: dry-toast whole seeds in a pan 1–2 min until they begin to pop and smell fragrant; cool completely; grind in Cecotec chopper or mortar. **Preferred grind for falafel: medium — not powder.** Small fragments of spice add character and texture.
- Whole seeds unground in the mixture: NOT recommended for falafel. Cumin seeds are small and hard — they create unpleasant sharp fragments.

**Substitutions:**
- Тмин (caraway): the closest botanical relative. Use ¾ the quantity. Slightly different profile — more European, less Middle Eastern. Not traditional but functional.
- Nothing else substitutes well. Cumin's specific volatile compounds (cuminaldehyde) have no close analogue in other common spices.

## Dry Spices — Кориандр (coriander seed)

Role: citrusy-floral counterpoint to cumin. Together they define the falafel spice profile.

Zone: MODERATE. ¾× is acceptable; ⁵⁄₄× shifts the balance noticeably.

**Critical note:** кумин and кориандр are used in equal quantities specifically because they balance each other — cumin is earthy/warm, coriander is citrusy/floral. If you adjust one, consider adjusting the other proportionally.

| Column | Verdict | Consequence |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 0      | Зира доминирует | Without coriander, cumin becomes oppressively earthy. The flavour is one-dimensional and heavy. |
| ½×     | Дисбаланс | Cumin outweighs coriander. The citrusy note is present but insufficient to balance. |
| ¾×     | Лёгкий дисбаланс | Noticeably more cumin-forward. Acceptable for those who prefer earthy over citrusy. |
| 1×     | Гармония ✓ | Earthy + citrusy in balance. The flavour is rounded and complex. |
| ⁵⁄₄×   | Цитрусовый перевес | Coriander starts to dominate. The flavour tips toward a citrusy-floral profile and loses the Middle Eastern character. |
| 3/2×   | Кориандр доминирует | Cumin is buried. The dish tastes more like something from a different regional cuisine. |

**Form variants:**
- Pre-ground: same caveats as cumin regarding freshness.
- Freshly ground from toasted seeds: same method as cumin. **Coriander seeds are softer and larger than cumin** — they can tolerate a slightly coarser grind. A medium-coarse grind for falafel is appropriate and adds texture.
- Whole seeds unground: POSSIBLE as an intentional textural element. Coriander seeds are softer than cumin and will not create unpleasant fragments. Not traditional in falafel, but edible and adds an interesting bite. Use only if intentional.

**Substitutions:**
- Fresh coriander leaf (кинза): NOT a drop-in replacement for the seed. The leaf and the seed have related but distinct flavour compounds. Do not substitute one for the other without adjusting quantities and expectations significantly.
- Small amount of ground cardamom (~¼ ч.л.) can add a citrusy-floral note in some contexts, but changes the flavour direction noticeably.
- Omission is better than a poor substitute — simply reduce cumin proportionally if coriander is unavailable.

## Salt

Role: flavour amplifier. Activates and lifts all other flavours. Without it, other ingredients taste muted regardless of their quantity.

Zone: NARROW in both directions. Salt is the most sensitive common ingredient by perceptible impact per gram.

| Column | Verdict | Consequence |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 0      | Всё приглушено | All flavours — spices, herbs, garlic — are noticeably muted. The dish tastes flat and dull. Everyone notices. |
| ½×     | Недосолено | Flavour is present but restrained. Spices don't fully express. Most people notice. |
| ¾×     | Слегка мало | Perceptibly underseasoned to an attentive palate. Acceptable to those who restrict salt. |
| 1×     | Баланс ✓ | Salt enhances without tasting salty. All other flavours come forward fully. |
| ⁵⁄₄×   | Слегка много | Saltiness is perceptible as its own note. Spices begin to taste sharper. Noticeable to most people. |
| 3/2×   | Пересолено | Salt dominates. Spices are overwhelmed. The dish is unpleasant. |

**Form variants:**
- Fine sea salt or table salt: standard for falafel. Dissolves evenly in the mixture.
- Крупная соль (coarse): dissolves unevenly in a raw mixture — not recommended unless ground first.
- Fleur de sel / хлопья соли: finishing salt only, not for mixing into raw mixtures.
- Соевый соус: introduces liquid and umami — not appropriate for falafel.

**Substitutions:** None that preserve the flavour-amplification function of salt. Reduce for dietary reasons knowing the flavour impact.

## Black Pepper — Чёрный перец

Role: background heat and complexity. Secondary seasoning that adds depth.

Zone: VERY WIDE. Most people cannot distinguish ½× from ⁵⁄₄× in a spiced mixture like falafel. This is the most forgiving ingredient in the recipe.

| Column | Verdict | Consequence |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 0      | Без тепла | Almost unnoticeable in a spiced mixture. Slightly simpler finish. |
| ½×     | Намёк | Barely perceptible warmth. |
| ¾×     | Лёгкое тепло | Present but subtle. Within broad tolerance zone. |
| 1×     | Лёгкое тепло ✓ | Gentle warmth in the finish. Adds complexity without asserting itself. |
| ⁵⁄₄×   | Заметнее | Pepper becomes slightly more assertive. Still pleasant for most. |
| 3/2×   | Перечная нота | Pepper is noticeable as its own flavour. Fine for pepper lovers; reduce if serving to people who dislike heat. |

**Form variants:**
- Молотый чёрный перец: standard.
- Свежемолотый: slightly more aromatic — minor improvement over pre-ground.
- Белый перец (white pepper): milder, less aromatic, same quantity.

**Substitutions:**
- Белый перец: same quantity, milder.
- Копчёная паприка: adds smokiness, not heat — changes the flavour direction rather than substituting.
- Кайенский перец: much hotter. Use ¼ the quantity.
- Omission: barely noticeable in a mixture with cumin and coriander.

## Leavening — Разрыхлитель (baking powder)

**CONTEXT NOTE:** Not traditional in falafel. Label as `УСЛОВНЫЙ` in the visualization.
Rationale for inclusion in air fryer recipes: hot oil в deep-fry creates explosive
steam lift from rapid moisture evaporation at the surface — this naturally aerates
the interior. An air fryer lacks this mechanism. A small amount of baking powder
partially compensates for this difference. It is a Western adaptation, not an error,
but it should be clearly labelled as such.

Zone: NARROW above 1×. Excess baking powder causes structural failure and off-flavour.

| Column | Verdict | Consequence |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 0      | Плотные — традиционно | Dense, heavy interior. Fully authentic — this is what traditional deep-fried falafel tastes like. In air fryer: acceptable but noticeably denser. |
| ½×     | Чуть плотнее | Marginal difference. |
| ¾×     | Почти как норма | Within tolerance zone for most people. |
| 1×     | Лёгче ✓ | Slightly lighter, more open crumb. Compensates partially for the air fryer's lack of explosive steam lift. |
| ⁵⁄₄×   | Рассыпаются | Balls become fragile — they may crack or break when turned or transferred. |
| 3/2×   | Разваливаются + привкус | Structural failure. Metallic/soapy aftertaste from excess baking powder. |

**Substitutions:**
- Сода (baking soda): use ¼ the quantity (~¼ ч.л. instead of 1 ч.л.). Requires an acid in the mixture to activate. Standard falafel has no acid — soda will not fully activate and may leave a metallic note. Only use if you also add lemon juice or yogurt.
- Omission: the traditional and correct choice.

## Binding Agent — Мука (flour)

**CONTEXT NOTE:** Not traditional in falafel. Label as `УСЛОВНЫЙ`.
The correct amount is NOT fixed — it is empirically determined by the ball test.
Mark the 1× column with `?` and explain that "1×" means "however much the ball test requires", not a fixed gram value.

When the mixture is correctly dried and correctly pulsed, no flour is needed.
Flour appears when: (a) the nут was not dried thoroughly enough, (b) the onion
released excess moisture, or (c) the food processor ran too long and the mixture
is too wet and paste-like.

| Column | Verdict | Consequence |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 0      | Рассыпается | Mixture does not bind. Balls fall apart immediately or disintegrate in the air fryer. (Exception: if technique was perfect, 0 flour is correct.) |
| ½×     | Хрупко | Holds shape when cold, cracks or crumbles when heated. |
| ¾×     | Держит форму | May be sufficient if mixture is only slightly wet. Test with the ball test. |
| 1×?    | Держит форму ✓ | "1×" = however much the ball test requires. Correct cohesion: ball holds shape at room temperature, does not stick to palm. |
| ⁵⁄₄×   | Плотнее | Slightly denser interior. Starting to taste floury. |
| 3/2×   | Мучнистые | Dense, doughy interior. Clear flour taste. The light crumbly texture of good falafel is lost. |

**Substitutions:**
- Нутовая мука (chickpea flour): same role, same quantity range. Enhances flavour coherence (all-chickpea dish). Slightly more hygroscopic — start with ½ the wheat flour amount and add gradually. Gluten-free.
- Кукурузный крахмал (cornstarch): use ½ the quantity — binds tighter with less bulk. Neutral flavour, no colour impact.
- Omission: the correct answer if technique is right. Always try zero flour first and test.
